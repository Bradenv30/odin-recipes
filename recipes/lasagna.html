Lasagna

Description

This is a recipe for gluten-free lasagna. You will need gluten free lasagna noodles, cottage cheese, tomato sauce, roasted garlic sauce, ground beef,and shredded mozzarella cheese.

Ingredients

  • 12 gluten free lasagna noodles
  • 24 oz 4% fat cottage cheese
  • 24 oz Rao's Tomato Basil Sauce
  • 12 oz Rao's Roasted Garlic Sauce
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef and drain liquid.
  2. Add tomato and garlic sauces to cooked ground beef.
  3. Par-boil lasagna noodles: boil some water in a large pot. Once it is boiling, put 4 noodles in for 3 minutes, then remove from water and place on seperate plate. Repeat with the next 4 noodles, and the remaining 4 noodles.
  4. Spray bottom of baking pan with oil. Place 4 noodles on the bottom. Spread about 1/3 of the cottage cheese over noodles. Spoon 1/3 of the meat sauce over the cottage cheese noodles, then put 1 cup of shredded mozerella cheese over the top.
  5. Repeat this 2 more times with the rest of the noodles, cottage cheese, meat sauce, and shredded mozerella cheese. You will have 3 layers.
  6. Cover the pan with aluminum foil and cook in over for about 40 minutes. After 40 minutes is done, remove the foil and cook for an additional 10-15 minutes.
  7. Remove from oven and let sit for at least 10 minutes before eating.
